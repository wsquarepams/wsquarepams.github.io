Blubbadoo, the discord bot.

A multipurpose bot for your server.

Commands: (ℹ️ - <...> means required and [...] means optional field.)


MODERATION COMMANDS:

==ban <user> <reason> - Ban people
==kick <user> <reason> - Kick people
==warn <user> <reason> - Warn people
==warnings [user] - See your or [user]'s warnings
==clearWarnings <user> - Clear the user's warnings (Does not work)
==perms <user> - See perms of that user (Note: USE_VAD = Use Voice Activity)
==purge <number of messages to purge> - Purge channels. You can purge a maximum of 2 week's worth or 100 messages.
==mute <member> - Mute a member.
==unmute <member> - Unmute a member.
==suggest <suggestion> - Post a suggestion in the channel you sent this in.
==role <add | remove> [user] <role name> - Add or remove the role from you or [user]. Do not use "@" when typing the role.

MONEY COMMANDS:

==money | bal | balance - Check your balance.
==dep|deposit <all | Number> - Deposit all or [Number] into your bank.
==withdraw <all | Number> - Withdraw all or [Number] from your bank.
==daily - Get your daily money. Cooldown: \`1d\`
==work - Work to get money. Cooldown: \`1h\`
==shop - View the shop.
==buy <itemName - Case sensitive> - Buy the item. itemName is case sensitive.
==hunt - Hunt for cash. 75% success rate. Requires a knife.
==rps <bet> - Play "ROCK PAPER SCISSORS" with the bot.
==math - Do a math question. $100 if correct, -$100 if wrong.
==coin <heads | tails> <bet> - Flip a coin and win your bet. Chance depends on your bet and total money. Requires a coin. Cooldown: \`30s\`
==rob <user> - Rob <user>. 20% success rate, earn 75% of their money, otherwise -50% of yours to them.
